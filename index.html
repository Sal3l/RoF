HISTORY

Roots of Flavor was born from the tradition of grandmother Eva, who in Poland transformed simple ingredients into unforgettable flavors. Inspired by her recipes and the warmth of her kitchen, we created a restaurant that combines Polish cuisine with modern touches, serving dishes that awaken memories and warm the heart.
